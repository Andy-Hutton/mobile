Theo Hutton

Theo is aged 5 years old and lives in West Kirby on the Wirral. Theo was starved of oxygen needing over seven minutes resuscitation and suffered a severe brain injury resulting in quadriplegic cerebral palsy. We want to help Theo reach his full potential and have an amazing quality of life by raising funds for life changing stem cell treatment and specialised equipment outside of the NHS, including intensive hyperbaric oxygen treatment. Theo is the biggest fighter we know with every little thing he does being a challenge. He has the biggest smile and is the happiest little boy who deserves a chance of the life he can enjoy just like everybody else. There are numerous therapies and pioneering treatments available, but they are not accessible on the NHS and the expense is very high. Not only will Theo need this treatment, he will also need specialist equipment. This is why we as Theo's parents and family want to launch 'Theo's Fight' Campaign.
